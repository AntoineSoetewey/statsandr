Below a Shiny app to help you read the main statistical tables:

  • Statistics-101

This Shiny app helps you to compute probabilities for the main probability distributions. How to use this app?

  1. Choose the distribution
  2. Set the paremeter(s) of the distribution
  3. Select whether you want to find the lower tail, upper tail or an interval
  4. Choose x

On the right panel (or below depending on the size of your screen) you will see:

  • a recap of the data you just entered
  • the numerical solution (i.e., the probability),
  • a visualization of the solution
  • the probability density function together with the mean, standard deviation and variance
